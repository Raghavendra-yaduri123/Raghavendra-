Customer Churn Prediction · Case Study

Goal: Predict churn for a telecom dataset and surface top drivers.

Stack: Python, Pandas, scikit-learn, imbalanced-learn.

Method: EDA → feature engineering (tenure bins, contract type dummies) → Logistic Regression with class weights; evaluated with ROC-AUC, F1.

Result: 0.82 F1 on validation; 5 key drivers identified for retention playbooks.
